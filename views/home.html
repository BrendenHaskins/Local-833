About Local 833

As the oldest (and only, so technically also the newest) beekeeping union - we lead the way for farmers all across the country to pollinate good crops, supply great honey, and empower the futures of our union's members. When you work with Local 833, you'll cultivate a rich background as an eco-friendly groundskeeper, beekeeper, relocater of bees, bee-storian (history of bees) and so much more!

Don't wait any longer! Application window closes soon! Put your strongest wing forward and take flight in your career today! Lead the future of honey supply, and save the bees!
